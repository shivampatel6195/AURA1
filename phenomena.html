Cosmos Phenomena

Explore the most fascinating space phenomena that have captivated scientists and astronomers for decades.

Black Holes

A region of space where gravity is so strong that nothing can escape from it, not even light.

For more detailed information, visit Black Holes on Wikipedia

Supernova

The explosion of a star, releasing an immense amount of energy.

For more detailed information, visit Supernovae on Wikipedia

Neutron Stars

The collapsed core of a massive star which is incredibly dense.

For more detailed information, visit Neutron Stars on Wikipedia

Pulsars

Highly magnetized rotating neutron stars that emit beams of electromagnetic radiation.

For more detailed information, visit Pulsars on Wikipedia

Quasars

Extremely bright and distant objects powered by black holes at the center of galaxies.

For more detailed information, visit Quasars on Wikipedia
